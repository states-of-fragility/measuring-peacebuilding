Tracking investments into Peacebuilding activities

This sankey diagram visualizes how expenditures directed towards Peacebuilding activities can be measured using the OECD's ODA Data. It is based on the peacebuilding framework proposed by the Institue for Peace and Economics in their report "Measuring Peacebuilding Cost-Effectiveness", 2017. The chords connect the activity areas with the respective purpose codes of the OECD Creditor Reporting System.
